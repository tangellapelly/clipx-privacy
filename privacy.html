Privacy Policy

ClipX · Last updated June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipX ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. We built ClipX to respect your privacy. In short: ClipX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information We Collect

None. ClipX does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, device identifiers, or diagnostic data. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not require you to provide any personal details to use the app.

How Your Data Is Handled

All app data, including any clipboard content managed by ClipX, stays on your device. It is never sent to us or to any third party. We have no servers that receive your data, and we have no ability to access the contents of your device.

Third-Party Services

ClipX does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties because no data leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

ClipX does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
